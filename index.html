Using AI to Support Publics

Google Jigsaw in Bowling Green, Kentucky, Mar 22, 2025

I was reading this article from Jigsaw, a Google organization focused on factuality and media. They deployed a "sensemaking" tool to help organizers capture community sentiment in a more granular way. While this use-case is interesting, we always have to wonder what the implications of technology are 10 years from now in the hands of less benevolent people.
